ABOUT US

B.C. Roy Engineering College, established in 2000, is a premier institution recognized for its excellence in engineering education and innovation. Ranked among the top engineering colleges in West Bengal, B.C. Roy offers state-of-the-art programs in disciplines like Computer Science, Electronics, and Mechanical Engineering. Situated on a sprawling campus with modern infrastructure, the college provides students with world-class facilities and exposure. With an impressive track record of student placements and industry tie-ups, including top offers from renowned companies, B.C. Roy Engineering College continues to nurture skilled professionals and future leaders in the field of engineering.

HORIZON: Innovate, Collaborate, Elevate! HORIZON is the premier tech fest of B.C. Roy Engineering College, where innovation meets creativity in an exhilarating celebration of technology and engineering. Over the course of three action-packed days, students come together to showcase their skills, explore new ideas, and push the boundaries of technology. Featuring a wide range of exciting events, from coding challenges and robotics competitions to hackathons and tech talks, HORIZON provides a platform for tech enthusiasts to collaborate, learn, and compete. It's not just a fest—it's a hub of innovation, where the brightest minds converge to shape the future.
